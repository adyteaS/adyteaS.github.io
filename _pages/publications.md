## Research Highlights

My research focuses on applying machine learning and computational methods to:

- **Foundation Models in Cancer Biology**: Developing and applying large-scale AI models for cancer research and drug discovery
- **Single Cell Genomics**: Predicting drug toxicity and cellular responses at single-cell resolution
- **Multi-omic Integration**: Combining diverse data types (genomics, transcriptomics, etc.) for comprehensive biological insights
- **Biomarker Discovery**: Identifying molecular markers for disease diagnosis and prognosis
- **Computer Vision for Infrastructure**: Applying deep learning to critical infrastructure monitoring and disaster prevention
