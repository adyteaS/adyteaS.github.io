Hello, I'm Aditi. I'm a Graduate Research Assistant in the Bioinformatics and Machine Learning Laboratory at The University of New Orleans.

In the University, I work on applying advanced machine learning techniques to real world problems in Computer Vision, Image Processing and Bioinformatics. Currently, I work on the detection of fault lines in Lake Pontchartrain by training a model with satellite images of surrounding areas. For my thesis, I am working on detecting water leakages near New Orleans' many levees. I co-authored a book chapter on Super-Secondary structures.

Along with this, I also assist the City of New Orleans, and UNO-CHART, a social sciences outreach program in collecting and analyzing data about houses in the New Orleans area for a Repetitive Flood Loss Project.

Previously, I worked as the Managing Editor for the University's weekly newspaper, Driftwood.

If you'd like to connect, feel free to email me.
